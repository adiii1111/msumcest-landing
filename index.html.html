About the Institution

A Legacy Rooted in Mindanao

From a community-founded high school to a fully integrated constituent of the MSU System — MSU-MCEST has grown for over 75 years.

The institution traces its roots to the Maigo High School, founded in School Year 1948–1949 under the Maigo Parent Teacher Association. It was converted into the Maigo Trade School under Republic Act No. 2408, and later integrated into the Mindanao State University System through MSU Board of Regents Resolution No. 11-A, s. 2001.

Today, MSU-MCEST reflects a modern academic identity — aligned with digital careers, teacher education, science, and technology for students across Lanao del Norte and the broader Mindanao region.

As part of the MSU System, MSU-MCEST shares the university's mandate: promoting quality education and advancing the development of Muslim and cultural minority communities in Mindanao.

How to Enroll

Admission Requirements

Prepare the following documents when applying. Requirements vary by applicant type.

Freshmen
  • HS Report Card / Form 138-A (Original)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • MSU-SASE or CET Report of Rating
  • 4 copies 2"×2" ID photo (name at back)
Transferees
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Evaluated Transcript of Records
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • MSU-SASE or CET Report of Rating
  • 4 copies 2"×2" ID photo
Second Coursers
  • Honorable Dismissal
  • Evaluated Transcript of Records
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Birth Certificate (PSA/NSO)
  • 4 copies 2"×2" ID photo
